Final Updates to Prototype 2 3/30/2016

Using the platform of Prototype 2, our device was able to successfully suction on the surface of the window while still locomoting. This was a step towards having the system finally able to reliably traverse the entirely of the window and clean the entire surface.


Initial Design of Prototype 2 3/16/2016

Our team debuted the next iteration of prototype at Milestone 3, which was a chassis that supported significantly more hardware than the prototype 1. This prototype was constructed of the same material (1/4" acrylic), but allowed for the mounting of sensors such as the ultrasonic sensors, and had better provisions for the limit switches. This system modularized the mounting for the different components such as the motors and the suction cup.


Proposal 1/17/2016

We made a proposal of our project, which consisted of our initial ideas and design choices. We described various design considerations and comparisions, the subsystems that would go into our project, and the parts that we are considering to build this proejct.
