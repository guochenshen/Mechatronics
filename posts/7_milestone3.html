Milestone 3 3/16/2016

Our team debuted the next iteration of prototype at Milestone 3, which was a chassis that supported significantly more hardware than the prototype 1. This prototype was constructed of the same material (1/4" acrylic), but allowed for the mounting of sensors such as the ultrasonic sensors, and had better provisions for the limit switches. This system modularized the mounting for the different components such as the motors and the suction cup. This allowed the work to progress in paralle, without the need to monopolize the device.

While the physical system was capable of handling all of the new sensors, it still was not yet integrated. The sensors were tested on an auxillary testbed, giving us distance and IMU readings.
