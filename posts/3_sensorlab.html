Sensor Lab 2/3/2016

Our team received a variety of sensors that we needed to obtain readings off of. This required using the data sheets of the sensors because we couldn't report the readings as voltages, but in physical units instead. The sensors that we were required to use were a IR sensor, ultrasonic sensor, a optical trigger, a light sensor, and a potentiometer.
