Milestone 6 5/3/2016

Our final prototype improved on our previous design, by utilizing a rigid, carbon fiber frame that allows our robotto withstand heavier crashes and falls. We improved on the manual power on switches and generally simplified wiring for the system to be easier to manage.

We recycled our suction system from Prototype 3 for the purposes for its modular installation process. Like prototype 3, we also chose to keep our obstacle traversal system removed in order to cut scope for the purposes of the final presentation.
