Milestone 5 4/27/2016

Prototype 3 involved a complete redesign of the base to allow for cleaner wiring, better suction, and an overall simpler design. Although we initially tried incorporating our obstacle traversal system, similar to that designed in Milestone 4, we found difficulty getting it to function properly.

This milestone also saw the eventual removal of our spring-loaded cleaning system for a simpler cleaning mechanism.
