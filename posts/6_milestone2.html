Milestone 2 3/2/2016

Milestone 2 marked the development phase where our device was capable of moving on its own using basic logic. This basic logic used the limit switches on the front of the device, and this enabled it to determine when it had reached the end of its path. Using this information, our device was capable of traversing a horizontal surface using a lawnmower pattern.

Unfortunately, our photographic documentation of this milestone was sparce and mostly took place in CAD and software. However, videos of this milestone can be found in our public Google Drive, showing the device moving in a lawnmower pattern.
