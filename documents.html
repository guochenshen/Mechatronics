Motor Lab 2/10/2016

For the motor lab, our team was tasked with figuring out how 3 different types of motors operated. We wrote the code for an Arduino, allowing us to control the motors with a GUI. The code incorporates PID control, various motor functions, as well as debouncing for a mechanical switch.


Sensor Lab 2/3/2016

For our product to accurately navigate its enviroment, it needs several sensors of different varieties and well written software to correctly receive and interpret the information. The sensor lab allowed us to use 5 different sensors with an Arduino to get readings of physical units using the datasheets from the sensor manufacturer.


Mock-Up 2/1/2016

The mockup, which consisted mostly of taped cardboard and scrap cans, demonstrated the overall layout of our project and how the sub-systems interacted with each other. We made facsimiles of the systems in our proposed design as the first step in determining the feasibility, as well as any egregious conflicts that we were not aware of.


Proposal 1/17/2016

We made a proposal of our project, which consisted of our initial ideas and design choices. We described various design considerations and comparisions, the subsystems that would go into our project, and the parts that we are considering to build this proejct.
